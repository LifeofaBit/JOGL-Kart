Free-View Mode:
W - Move forward (zoom-in)
S - Move back (zoom-out)
A - Move left
D - Move right
R - Move up
F - Move down
Mouse click and drag rotates look

Racing Mode:
W - Accelerate
S - Braking/Reverse
A - Turn Left
D - Turn Right
T - Toggle Camera between first person, third person, and mode to look at rotation of tires
Space Bar - Pauses game, initialized free-view mode
